## Mission (non-negotiable ship gate)

> **North star:** Build **Haven** — a unified operations platform for **assisted living facilities**, **home health**, and **home- and community-based care** — so multi-site, multi-entity operators can run clinical workflows, compliance, workforce, family engagement, and business operations on **one secure, role-governed data layer**. Improve **resident safety and quality**, **regulatory readiness**, **staff clarity**, and **owner visibility**. Use **AI** to reduce administrative burden and surface risk early; it must remain **subordinate to human judgment, licensure rules, and auditability**.

When planning or reviewing work, state **mission alignment** (`pass` | `risk` | `fail`) and ensure gate artifacts exist before treating a segment as complete.
