# 12 Training And Competency

- Spec maturity: `PARTIAL`
- Repo posture: training hubs, completion logging, and in-service flows are shipped

## What It Covers

Training catalog, mandatory competency tracking, completion logs, and in-service session management.

## Primary Users

- Facility admins
- Managers
- Compliance and training coordinators

## Key Workflows

- define or review training programs
- log individual staff completions
- record in-service sessions and attendee participation
- monitor expiring or missing competencies

## Primary Surfaces

- `/admin/training`
- `/admin/training/new`
- `/admin/training/completions/new`
- `/admin/training/inservice/new`

## Data, Controls, And Automation

- training programs, completions, and in-service attendance models
- Florida mandatory training requirements as a seeded context
- explicit support for external competency issuers such as Baya

## Deck Framing

- Position this as workforce readiness and licensure protection.
- Show that competency is tracked as an operational system, not a spreadsheet.
