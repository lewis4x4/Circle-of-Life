# 10 Quality Metrics

- Spec maturity: `PARTIAL + COL notes`
- Repo posture: quality hub exists, but the broader quality operating model is still evolving

## What It Covers

The quality and outcomes layer for facility-level scorecards, measures, composite scoring, improvement projects, and satisfaction signals.

## Primary Users

- Organization leadership
- Quality teams
- Facility operators improving outcomes

## Key Workflows

- define and view quality measures
- aggregate outcome metrics across facilities
- support quality improvement and PDSA-style work
- connect satisfaction and operational metrics into one quality view

## Primary Surfaces

- `/admin/quality`
- `/admin/quality/measures/new`

## Data, Controls, And Automation

- quality score and composite concepts
- improvement project framing
- links to incidents, infection, compliance, and family experience

## Deck Framing

- Treat this as the “are we improving?” layer above day-to-day operations.
- It works best as a bridge between clinical modules and executive reporting.
